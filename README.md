## Who I am
I'm Jyot Patel, a 14-year-old living in the United States. I recently moved from Canada, where I was raised my entire life (born in India though). I like tinkering and making software projects in my free time, though I never really used GitHub that often. Git CLI just wasn't for me.

## What is this project?
This is the core foundation of my handheld device. It contains the power circuitry and the main GPS module that handles everything. At the heart of it sits an RPI CM4 module, which controls everything and runs the main OS I plan on flashing to the SD card.

## Why did I take on such an ambitious project for my first PCB?
Honestly, it was kind of a bad idea for a first PCB, but I do have experience with real hardware. It was more of a motivational thing: when you want to give up, you realize how much you've already put into the project, and that pushes you to keep going. This was my 4th revision of the board, so as you can see, it took me a while to get here.

## The routing looks ugly
I can admit the routing is terrible, but I checked it for the specific purpose and this will work just fine. I wasn't planning on making a Mona Lisa on a PCB. I just needed a functional board that won't explode after 3 years.
